How can we help?

Welcome to the Winker.io support page. If you encounter any issues or have questions, check out the frequently asked questions below or contact us directly.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login screen and click on "Forgot Password." Follow the instructions to reset your password via email.

2. How can I report a bug?

If you encounter a bug, please email us at support@winker.io with a description of the issue, and we'll look into it right away.

3. Is my data secure?

Yes, we prioritize your privacy and security. All your data is encrypted and handled according to best practices.
